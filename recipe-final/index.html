Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Seven step recipe

A level up from your average chocolate chip cookie! Prep Time: 2 hrs 20 mins Cook Time: 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups dark brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg, 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon plain greek yogurt
  • 3/4 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 3/4 cup milk chocolate chips
  • Maldon sea salt

Step 1

Brown the butter. Add butter to a large saucepan and place over medium heat. Make sure to whisk constantly. When the butter changes color, remove pan from heat and transfer to a bowl.

Step 2

Using an electric mixer, mix the brown butter and sugars until they are combined. Beat in the egg, yolk, vanilla extract, and yogurt until smooth.

Step 3

In a separate bowl, whisk the dry ingredients together. Then add them to the wet ingredients and beat on low-speed. Add in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed into the dough.

Step 4

Chill your dough for 2 hours in the refrigerator.

Step 5

Remove dough from the refrigerator and roll into small balls. Place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

Step 6

Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Once ready, bake cookies for 9-11 minutes, or until golden brown.

Step 7

Cool the cookies on the baking sheets for at least 5 minutes. Sprinkle each cookie with a pinch of sea salt.
